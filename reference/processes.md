## Naming logic

- **`-genesis`** = *making*: building or storing
- **`-lysis`** = *breaking down*: release or burning

---

## Complete table

| Process | What it does | Substrate -> Product | Activates when | Insulin |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Glycolysis** | Burns glucose for energy | Glucose -> ATP + CO2 + H2O | Always, more during effort | -- |
| **Glycogenesis** | Stores glucose as glycogen | Glucose -> Glycogen | After meals, glucose surplus | High |
| **Glycogenolysis** | Releases glucose from glycogen | Glycogen -> Glucose | Between meals, effort, fasting | Low |
| **Lipogenesis (DNL)** | Turns surplus into fat | Glucose -> Triglycerides | Full glycogen, calorie surplus | High |
| **Esterification** | Stores fat in adipocytes | Fatty acids -> Triglycerides | High insulin, fat in blood | High |
| **Lipolysis** | Releases fat from storage | Triglycerides -> Fatty acids + glycerol | Low insulin, deficit, effort | Low |
| **Beta-oxidation** | Burns fatty acids for energy | Fatty acids -> ATP + CO2 + H2O | Low insulin, energy need | Low |
| **Gluconeogenesis** | Makes glucose from other sources | Amino acids/glycerol -> Glucose | Fasting, empty glycogen, intense effort | Low |
| **Protein synthesis** | Builds muscle and organs | Amino acids -> Structural proteins | Protein intake + effort stimulus | Moderate |
| **Digestive proteolysis** | Digests dietary protein | Protein -> Amino acids | Every meal with protein | -- |
| **Muscle proteolysis** | Breaks down muscle | Muscle -> Amino acids | Empty pool + low protein | -- |
| **Ketogenesis** | Produces alternative fuel | Fatty acids -> Ketone bodies | Prolonged fasting, keto | Low |
| **Deamination** | Processes excess amino acids | Amino acids -> Carbon skeleton + Urea | Protein surplus | -- |

---

## Grouped by mode

### STORAGE mode: high insulin

```text
Glycogenesis      -> glucose -> GLYCOGEN
Lipogenesis       -> glucose -> FAT
Esterification    -> fatty acids -> TRIGLYCERIDES in adipocytes
Protein synthesis -> amino acids -> MUSCLE
```

### BURNING mode: low insulin

```text
Glycogenolysis  -> GLYCOGEN -> glucose -> energy
Lipolysis       -> FAT -> fatty acids -> energy
Beta-oxidation  -> fatty acids -> ATP + CO2 + H2O
```

### EMERGENCY mode: empty glycogen

```text
Gluconeogenesis    -> amino acids/glycerol -> new glucose
Ketogenesis        -> fat -> ketone bodies (brain fuel)
Muscle proteolysis -> MUSCLE -> amino acids -> glucose  avoid
```

## Key enzymes

| Enzyme | Role | Controlled by |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **LPL** (lipoprotein lipase) | Puts fat **INTO** adipocytes | Insulin **activates** it |
| **HSL** (hormone-sensitive lipase) | Pulls fat **OUT OF** adipocytes | Insulin **blocks** it |
| **GLUT4** | Transports glucose into muscle | Insulin + muscle contraction |
| **Glucose-6-phosphatase** | Allows glucose to be sent into blood | Present in **liver**, not muscle |
