## Storage and Burning Cycle

```text
YOU EAT
carbohydrates, fats
        |
        v
BLOOD GLUCOSE RISES
INSULIN RISES
        |
        v
STORAGE MODE
LPL active; HSL blocked
        |
        +--> GLYCOGEN
        |    liver + muscle
        |
        +--> ADIPOCYTES
             stored fat
             |
             v
TIME / FASTING / EFFORT
Blood glucose drops
Insulin drops
        |
        v
BURNING MODE
HSL active; LPL inactive
        |
        +--> GLYCOGEN -> glucose -> ATP
        |
        +--> FAT -> fatty acids
                  |
                  v
              BETA-OXIDATION
              fatty acids -> ATP
              final products: CO2 + H2O
```

## Glucose Destinations by Priority

```text
YOU EAT carbohydrates
        |
        v
GLUCOSE in blood
        |
        +--> #1 ENERGY NOW -> ATP
        |    brain, organs, ~120g/day
        |
        +--> #2 GLYCOGEN
        |    liver (~100g) + muscle (~400g)
        |    if full -> #3 FAT
        |
        +--> #3 FAT
             adipocytes, only surplus
```

---

## The Two Glycogen Stores

```text
LIVER GLYCOGEN
- Location: liver, ~100g
- Empties: overnight, between meals
- Feeds: the whole body through blood
- Can send glucose into blood: yes

MUSCLE GLYCOGEN
- Location: muscle, ~300-400g
- Empties: only during physical effort
- Feeds: only that specific muscle
- Can send glucose into blood: no
- Missing enzyme: glucose-6-phosphatase
```

## Fat Path

```text
YOU EAT fat
        |
        v
DIGESTION
fatty acids + glycerol
        |
        v
REASSEMBLY
triglycerides in chylomicrons
        |
        v
LYMPH -> BLOOD -> ADIPOCYTES
        |
        +--> HIGH INSULIN
        |    LPL active -> storage by esterification
        |
        +--> LOW INSULIN
             HSL active -> lipolysis -> fatty acids
                           |
                           v
                       beta-oxidation -> ATP + CO2 + H2O
```
